About

This is the farm system developed at The Njoroges Farm.

For a start, the system was used to keep records of the Mushroom growing process. This was sparked by diverse, incomplete records kept by individual farmers. Some did not have records and used estimation. However, record keeping is an important part of every business, even in agriculture.

The aim of timsystem, as we call it, is to have a central database. The aim at the end is to leverage the Big data advantage of processing data. Therefore, this system was first used for out first Mushroom crop, 1-B1, of 2019.
